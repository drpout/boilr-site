Features

Boilr is an Android app which monitors Bitcoin, cryptocurrencies and bullion prices, and triggers alarms according to user settings.

Price Hit

Triggered when price crosses the alarm's upper or lower limit.

Price Change

Triggered when price changes more than X amount (in currency or percentage) in a specified time frame (e.g. 15 min). It uses a rolling time frame: price is fetched with a given update interval (e.g. 30 s) and compared with the price fetched one time frame ago (e.g. 15 min ago).

Triggered alarms ring, displaying their status, until you turn them off. Just like an alarm clock.

Sound and vibration are configurable globally and individually for each alarm.

Delete alarms with a swipe.

Reorder alarms after a long click.

Lists of exchanges, pairs and alarms are fully searchable, allowing a quick alarm setup and configuration.

Market data is retrieved directly from the exchanges via Wi-Fi or Mobile Data (if allowed). Boilr supports all exchanges available on libdynticker, and all their pairs.

Supports multiple languages: Chinese, Czech, French German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese and Spanish.

There is an alternative light theme.
